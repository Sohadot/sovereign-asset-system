**The Shovel Economy Reference System**

ShovelsSale.com is not a literal shovel store, a generic blog, an ecommerce concept, an affiliate surface, or a short-term domain-sale landing page.

Its long-term role is to become a trusted reference system for classifying where value is extracted, enabled, accumulated, and controlled inside gold-rush markets.
