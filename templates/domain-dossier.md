# Domain Dossier

## Purpose

This dossier is the unified working document for a single domain or strategic digital asset inside the Sovereign Asset System.

Its purpose is to gather the most important strategic layers of the asset into one disciplined operating file.

It should make the asset legible across evaluation, narrative, buyer logic, commercialization posture, engagement posture, and next actions.

This dossier is not a loose note file.

It is the canonical working brief for the asset.
